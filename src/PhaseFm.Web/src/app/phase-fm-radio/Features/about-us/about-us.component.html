Who We Are

Phase FM is an online radio station that broadcasts worldwide. The station studio is located in Mdantsane, Buffalo City Metropolitan Municipality, Eastern Cape Province in South Africa.

The station brings and provides fresh, innovative, unique and fascinating radio format of programmes that cater for young and old listeners.
