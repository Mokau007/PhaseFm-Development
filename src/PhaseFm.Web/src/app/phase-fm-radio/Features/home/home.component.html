About Us

Flores is small business that provides giftboxes to its customers.
The giftboxes available are fresh flower boxes, satin boxes,
mixed boxes and custom boxes. Customers send a message on one
of her social media platforms detailing what they want then Menka
sends them an invoice and once payment is received the order will
be made and sent out.

Menka is currently working alone but is looking into hiring employees.
